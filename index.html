Hola from rural Andalucia and welcome to my website and blog

You are probably here because you like food in which case you will be as well to bookmark this page so that you can return for recipes, ideas, a wry smile and maybe even inspiration.

I love cooking with herbs and freshly ground spices in a relaxed and unhurried manner. Food should be cooked with a little love even when cooking for one, don’t you think?

You can follow me virtually by way of my blog, Twitter, Facebook and other social media but if you want to experience real Spain in person then I can offer Cookery school and Walking the Via Verde located under Visiting or call by if you are passing – there’s always a chilled something in the fridge and the view from the roof terrace is fabulous. Just fabulous.

Hasta Luego!

NEW FOR 2016 - Holiday Villa with a Personal Chef

Casa Rural El Villar is located near the town of Rute in Andalusia’s province of Córdoba. Rute is famous for its production of olives, anise, jamons and boasts the largest chocolate nativity scene in the world. El Villar is also close to the man-made lake at Iznájar where there is a beach and you can swim. There is also a water sports centre.

The property has been reformed and kept in the Andaluz style complete with Spanish and Mexican furniture. It is comfortable, spacious and typically Spanish.

El Villar provides, The Flexible Chef, your own personal chef for the duration of your stay.
